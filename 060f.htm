Gamecheats 


 * Eggs:
       * Classic 2600 Pitfall:
            1. At the main menu, select "Info", press the C button 26 times,
               then press Down.
            2. Or at the start of the "Copan" level, get to the very top
               platform, then do a running jump to the left to land on the
               ledge with the 2600 version scorpion. Enter the door to a
               ledge with another scorpion, a lock, and silver bar. Go left
               to hit the wall, jump up & down till catapulted in the air.
               Go right while in the air and land in a small room with a
               colorful vortex. Enter the vortex.
