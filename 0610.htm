Tips zu FlipOut


Gamecheats

1) The Programmer's Heads!
On any level that features the three cheerleader aliens, type (slowly & carefully) 14283722. If you typed it correctly, The music should change, an evil laugh should play repeatedly and the Cheerleaders should change into the three main programmers for the game.

2) Automatically Win Level!
7798899. Self Explanitory.

3) Clear all levels on Map screen!
779889#. Ditto

Enjoy, and remember that you must key these in very carefully.
Even the dudes that made the game have to try several times.
