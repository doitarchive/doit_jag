7.5 SpaceAce


Tips zu Space Ace


Gamecheats 


General Tips:  Timing is critical, a move made too early or too late will end
your mission.  Usually you should only press your move once. While playing
through some scenes however, you may press your move many times before the
action in order to insure the execution of the move. Several scenes may appear
flipped, left to right. For these scenes, substitute left for right, and right
for left in the following hints.

"Kim is Kidnapped"

The correct moves for Kimmy's Capture are:
Ace: right, left, left, (energize), fire, fire, right, left, right, fire, left,
left, left, left, left

Kimmy's capture may also be played with an alternate path.
The correct moves for Kimmy's capture alternate are:
Dex: right, left, left (do not energize), left, left, left, left

"The Black Sheres"

The correct moves for the Chase are:
Long: up, right, up, fire, right, fire, down, fire, up, left, up, fire, left,
fire, down, fire

The Chase also may be flipped.
The Chase also may be played with an Alternate path.
The correct moves for the Chase Alternate are:
Quick: up, right, down, right, up, up, left, down, left, up

"Dodging The Beams"

The correct moves for Borf's ship are:
Ace: up, left, up, up, (energize), fire, left, fire, up, up, fire, left, left

Borf's ship also may be flipped.
Borf's ship may also be played with an alternate path.
The correct moves for Borf's ship alternate are:
Dex: up, left, up, up (do not energize), up, up, up, left, left

"The Platforms"

The correct moves for Dex in the Platform Room are:
Ace: right, up, (energize), fire, fire, right, fire, fire, right, fire, fire,
right

The Platform room also may be flipped.
Dex: left, right, left, up, left, left

"The Dogs and Robots"

The Rocket Launching Site may also be played with an alternate path.
The correct moves for Launching Site Alternate are:
Dex: up, right, left (do not energize), up, left, right, up, left

Garbage planet may also be played with an alternate path.
